Frequently Asked Questions

  • We truly mean "unlimited." We don't limit the number of edits you can ask us to make. There is, however, an extra fee for extra pages or feature beyond the "5-Page standard website".

  • When you sign up with us, we expect a 12 month commitment. After the 12 months, you can cancel any time. We will transfer the domain to you, but the website will be taken down.

  • If you'd like to add a page to your website, we will gladly help out! We charge a $100 one time fee for each additional page.

  • Typically, a 5 page website includes a home page, about page, services page, contact page and an FAQ page. But in reality we can build whatever 5 pages you need.

  • If you have a logo already, awesome! We can use that on your website. If you'd like us to make a new one, we can do that too. It's all included in the package deal.
